Who we are


Imagalgo has been designed as a tool to help students better understand data structures and algorithms, by allowing them to learn and imagine the basics on their own and at their own pace.




Open-book

Imagalgo is free of charge for Computer Science community on earth.Our centre aim is to aware you with every familiar as well as non-familiar existing algorithms.




Visualization

Imagalgo contains visual representation of many algorithms that are widely discussed in course curriculum of data structures and algorithms.




Course

Imagalgo is an on-going project that provides flexibility for students to learn from any device eg.smartphones etc due to its responsive nature and help in imagining the procedures .
